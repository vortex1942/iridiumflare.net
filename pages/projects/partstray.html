Modular parts tray

Project Overview

This was a 2 hour design project, To make a parts tray with interchangable screw holders, The only purchased parts are a Petri dish set, which brings the cost to $4.5AUD per tray. vs Commercial trays which are $20 each

Design Challenges

  • Keeping a lower cost than commercial versions
  • Transparant top for visibility.
  • Reduce the risks of losing screws

Remaining Work

  • Make the model fully parametric
  • Make additional screw plates
  • Publish design
